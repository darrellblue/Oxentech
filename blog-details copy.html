The Metaverse: WILL YOU JOIN

I most recently became obsessed with the Metaverse over the last month after watching Mark Zuckerberg announcing that Facebook would be changing its name to Meta and will start rolling out applications and investing to steak its claim in the Metaverse. I'm sure if you have any interest in the Tech sector you have heard this word a lot lately; Metaverse. Now trying to wrap your head around the concept of the Metaverse might be kind of complicated especially because it doesn't entirely exist just yet. It is unclear if the Metaverse will be one or many universes but what we do know is that it will be an immersive version of the next-generation internet likely rendered by augmented reality glasses while using such devices like Quest.

One of the ways the companies plans to use Metaverse is to recruit and manage personnel. Companies such as Hyundai have used the Zepeto virtual world application to recruit new employees. According to reports, Samsung will hold a virtual job fair through a platform called Gather in September. ...So far, Metaverse has been limited to virtual gaming platforms, but companies like Meta are planning to integrate it into our lives in the same way as social media. Supporters of Metaverse imagine that its users can work, play, and keep in touch with friends in a variety of activities, from concerts and conferences to virtual trips around the world. By focusing on smart glasses, virtual reality headsets, and realistic Internet video calls, Meta hopes to go beyond its two-dimensional social media platform list and enter an interactive digital environment called Metaverse.

Its Metaverse will include current Meta products such as WhatsApp and Messenger, but it will also provide businesses and content creators with many new tools, such as virtual homes, offices, and e-commerce opportunities. In the imagination of Meta's metaverse, users can completely creatively control their virtual world, designing everything from beach houses to space stations, where you can collaborate, chat with friends, or study. Using VR, AR, and our most advanced technology tools, Metaverse will connect the physical world and the digital world. In addition, Metaverse will have its own economy and support a digital life that is almost as rich as the real world.

Mark Zuckerberg described the meta-universe as an interconnected digital space in which you can experience new experiences through virtual avatars, no matter where you are. Metaverse is a shared virtual space in which we can all use virtual and augmented reality devices to live, work, play, and interact. Although Meta may say that the virtual world is a new and exciting experience, others believe that Metaverse exists through platforms such as Discord. Discord was originally a place for gamers s relax online. Video games such as Fortnite and Roblox and virtual social worlds such as Decentraland can already be considered as part of Metaverse.

Many people see the metaverse not as a single world, but as a scenario in which we can seamlessly transfer ourselves and our things - from fashion to art to the grocery store - from platform to platform. The Metaverse is usually portrayed as a kind of “donned” digital Internet - a manifestation of real reality, but based in a virtual world (often similar to a theme park), such as those depicted in Ready Player One and The Matrix. Writer Neil Stevenson is credited with coining the term "metaverse" in his 1992 science fiction novel Snow Crash, in which he introduced realistic avatars found in realistic 3D buildings and other virtual reality environments. While the company formerly known as Facebook is unlikely to take on the dystopian tones of Stevenson's novel, their vision of the metaverse roughly approaches that concept: a shared, connected virtual world in which you can study, work, shop, meet people, play and create.

Given the resources available to today's tech giants, they are likely to have a significant impact on the development and evolution of the metaverse. However, Carr still believes there will be plenty of room for alternative social worlds due to how the metaverse is likely to be structured and governed. Another risk, says EQT's Ted Persson, is that the metaverse remains too fragmented for people to be unable to migrate their virtual identities across platforms. A battle is brewing to determine whether the metaverse will be run by a company like Facebook or whether multiple companies will collaborate.

At the same time, massively multiplayer online games like Fortnite, Minecraft (owned by Microsoft), and Roblox have become the mainstream of social games and have created platforms that can be used as the cornerstone of the development of the meta-universe. Some game developers believe that Roblox—yes, this boxy game loved by children between 7 and 12—may be the most powerful platform for Metaverse in the future. Roblox is an online children's game, which is generally regarded as a pioneer of Metaverse because players can enter the game world through virtual reality headsets, computers, tablets, or mobile phones. The platform was established in 2004 and contains dozens of custom games, including RPG products such as Bloxburg and Brookhaven, where users can build houses, work, and create scenes.

Basically, this is a world of endless, interconnected virtual communities where people can use virtual reality headsets, augmented reality glasses, smartphone apps, or other devices to meet, work, and play. As the Metaverse expands, it will provide a surreal alternative world that can coexist. In online gaming worlds such as Fortnite, Minecraft, and Roblox, metaverse hints already exist.

Zuckerberg described a virtual world in which consumers will not only buy hardware to access the meta world but also have a variety of shopping opportunities in the meta world. Users can purchase new products made for their avatars. Everything from clothes to retail stores. Metaverse is a set of new technologies that many providers will create, and now this is a race to get more added value at a faster rate. If Meta gets along well, Metaverse can open the door to new work, social, and entertainment opportunities that will affect your daily life. The social coordination, infrastructure construction, and technological advancement required to create a universal meta-universe like the one depicted in science fiction are immense.

So now the question is asked; will you join the Metaverse or do you feel that this technology is will create more of a social disconnect on our society? Lets wait and see what future holds for the Metaverse.

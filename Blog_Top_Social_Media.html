Top Social Media Trends Of 2022 For Small Businesses

Today, we've put together a breakdown of popular social media trends and features across platforms that you can leverage to better market your brand and small business in 2022. Follow the latest social media and marketing trends to help businesses stand out in a digitally overloaded world while attracting customers.

Technology to capture the attention of online users continues to proliferate in 2022, and leveraging popular trends on various social platforms is a great way for marketers to reach their target audience and build brand awareness among new audiences. To keep up with social media trends in 2022 and beyond, brands need to start thinking about how to personalize their ads based on user behavior, even beyond social platforms. Here's a social media trend we'll see in 2022 and beyond, as social platforms find new ways to monetize their sites, which means brands will have to keep up with more innovative advertising if they want a piece of the pie.

With growing social media trends focusing on video and graphic content, it's no surprise that Instagram will see the majority of social media ad spend. Short videos will continue to dominate the web in 2022, and as a result, most social media trends will be video-based. According to Cisco research, by 2022, 82% of all online content will be video.

The explosive growth of TikTok is one of the reasons shortened video content will become even more popular in 2022. As mentioned, live streaming has become more popular in recent years, but stories and other shortened video content are also available. 2022. The popularity of video is only increasing as major social media platforms such as Facebook, Twitter, and LinkedIn improve their video capabilities to keep up with rising popularity and new trends.

Be sure to incorporate some of these video platforms into your 2022 branding and marketing strategy. If you're looking for a cost-effective strategy to cut down on the noise and grab the attention of your target market on social media in 2022, it's time to partner with your brand's fans and supporters.

Incorporating social media into your online marketing efforts can help you expand your reach and grow your audience. In fact, according to Google, adding new social channels to your marketing mix can increase your return on investment (ROI) by a staggering 35%. As a SMB marketer, gaming-related ads may not be available right now, but as Facebook and other major platforms continue to roll out branding tools for their latest features, it's no surprise that more ads in social media gaming experiences are possible in the future.
